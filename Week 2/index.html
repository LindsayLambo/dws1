The Vantage

The V12 Vantage S is considered an extreme sports car and with a top
speed of 205 miles per hour, Aston Martin was not kidding around. With
a 0-60 time of 3.7 seconds, running late should never be an issue. Housing a new AM28 engine, which is a 6.0L that provides a marvelous
565 base horsepower, this is a car that is on many, many wish lists.
